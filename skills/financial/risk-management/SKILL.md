# Risk Management

## Purpose
Manages financial risks through quantitative analysis, modeling, and mitigation strategies.

## When to Use
- Evaluating investment portfolios for potential risks
- Ensuring compliance with financial regulations
- Performing stress tests on financial models

## Key Capabilities
- Identify and assess market, credit, and operational risks
- Calculate Value at Risk (VaR) and other metrics
- Develop and implement risk mitigation strategies
